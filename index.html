The Game

Three Rules of Survival

Up to a full lobby drops into the carnival. One of you is the killer. The rest have one job: don't be found long enough to get out.

01

Disguise

Become anything — a pumpkin, a barrel, a teddy bear, a trash can. Real-time prop physics means every object in the carnival is a place to hide.

02

Repair

Sneak out of cover to fix the generators and power the carnival back up. Nail the skill-checks — slip up and the whole map hears the bang.

03

Escape

With the power on, the exit gates open. Make your run for freedom — or stay hidden and pray The Butcher picks someone else first.

The Killer

The ButcherOne axe. No mercy.

He doesn't run the carnival — he haunts it. Every still object could be breathing. He listens for the careless, traps the predictable, and sees through the disguise of anyone who lingers too long. Swing the axe, hunt the props, and make sure nobody leaves.
